Hershey's Chocolate Cake

A rich super moist chocolate cake with a chocolate buttercream frosting. This is the best cake in the world!

Recipe by Blancdeblanc Updated on February 19, 2023

Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 30 mins Total Time: 1 hr Servings: 12 Yield: 1 (4-layer) 9-inch cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Frosting:
    • ¾ cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
    • 5 ⅓ cups confectioners' sugar
    • 1 ½ cups unsweetened cocoa powder
    • ⅔ cup milk
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Step 1 Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. Step 2 Make cake: Stir together sugar, flour, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  3. Step 3 Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; mix for 3 minutes with an electric mixer. Stir in boiling water by hand.
  4. Step 4 Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  5. Step 5 Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  6. Step 6 Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the centers comes out clean, 30 to 35 minutes. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans to cool completely.
  7. Step 7 While cakes cool, make frosting: Cream butter with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Stir in confectioners' sugar and cocoa alternately with milk and vanilla. Beat to a smooth spreading consistency.
  8. Step 8 Split the layers of the cooled cake horizontally, cover the top of each layer with frosting, then stack them onto a serving plate.
  9. Step 9 Frost the outside of the cake with remaining frosting.
  10. Step 10 Enjoy!
