Milk Cake (Tres Leches)

This delicious tres leches cake features three types of milk: whole milk, condensed milk, and evaporated milk. It's topped with sweet whipped cream. An excellent cake for milk lovers!

Recipe by Stephanie Updated on November 28, 2022

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 30 mins Total Time: 1 hr Servings: 24 Yield: 1 9x13-inch loaf

Ingredients

  • ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • teaspoon baking powder
  • cup unsalted butter
  • cup white sugar
  • eggs
  • teaspoon vanilla extract
  • cups whole milk
  • (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 1 ½ cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 ½ cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Step 1 Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. Step 2Sift flour and baking powder together; set aside.
  3. Step 3 Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla; beat well. Add flour mixture, about 1/2 cup at a time, mixing until well blended. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  4. Step 4 Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30 minutes. Pierce cake all over with a fork; let cool to room temperature.
  5. Step 5 Mix whole milk, condensed milk, and evaporated milk together in a bowl; pour over the top of the cooled cake and allow to soak in.
  6. Step 6 Whip cream, remaining 1 cup of sugar, and 1 teaspoon vanilla in a chilled glass or metal bowl with an electric mixer until thick. Spread over the top of the filling. Keep cake refrigerated until serving.
  7. Step 7 Enjoy
